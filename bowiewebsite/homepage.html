David Bowie

On January 8, 1947, one of the most inspirational singers the world has ever known was born. Born in Brixton, London, David Robert Jones, also known as David Bowie, grew up being inspired by the sights and sounds of the London streets. In 1969, Bowie released his first studio album, Space Oddity, which was a homage to the kaleidoscopic influences of the London art scene. In 1972, Bowie released The Rise and Fall of Ziggy Stardust and the his persona of Ziggy Stardust had emerged and risen to fame. The eccentric, alien-looking persona that was inspired by Japanese theater and interstellar sci-fi, is what created the wave of what we now know as glam rock.
